Clinical services

What we do

Our work includes mental health assessments, neurodevelopmental assessments, addiction psychiatry, treatment recommendations, medication reviews where clinically appropriate, and reports for individuals, families, employers, insurers, legal representatives and professional bodies.

Mental health assessments

Comprehensive psychiatric assessment for a wide range of mental health concerns, including diagnostic review, formulation, treatment planning and recommendations for ongoing care.

Neurodevelopmental assessments

Assessment for neurodevelopmental conditions, including attention deficit hyperactivity disorder and autism spectrum disorder, with clear written outcomes and practical recommendations.

Addiction psychiatry

Specialist assessment and treatment planning for alcohol, substance use and behavioural addiction concerns, with a focus on risk, recovery, relapse prevention and wider mental health needs.

Treatment and care planning

Clinical treatment recommendations, medication review where appropriate, liaison with other professionals and practical guidance for next steps.

Who we support

Adults, young people, families and organisations

Bird Healthcare supports adults, young people, families and organisations who need specialist psychiatric input, clear clinical judgement and practical written guidance.

  • Private psychiatric assessments
  • Second opinions
  • Occupational reports
  • Medico legal reports
  • Diagnostic clarification
  • Treatment planning
Reports and documentation

Clear, structured reports

We provide clear, structured reports for private individuals, families, employers, insurers, legal representatives and other professional bodies.

Every assessment is designed to understand the whole person, not just a presenting symptom. Our aim is to provide practical answers, safe recommendations and clear documentation that can be understood by patients, families and professionals.
